## About This Assessment

This assessment is based on the integrative fit framework from Liu et al. (2024), which measures person-occupation fit across five key domains:

1. **Interests** - What activities you enjoy
2. **Values** - What you find important in work
3. **Knowledge** - What you know or want to learn
4. **Skills** - What you can do
5. **Personality** - How you prefer to work

Wage data is sourced from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics (OEWS) survey.
