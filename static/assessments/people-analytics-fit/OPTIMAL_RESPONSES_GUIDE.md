## Expected Fit Score with These Responses

With these optimal responses, you should achieve approximately **95-98% fit** with the People Analyst occupation. The exact score depends on:
1. How closely your averaged domain scores match the occupation profile
2. The correlation calculation (which uses standardized scores)
3. Rounding effects from integer responses

## Notes on Achieving Maximum Score

1. **Pattern Matching Matters More Than Exact Values**: Pearson correlation measures the pattern, so having the right relative differences is more important than exact matches.

2. **Domain Averages**: Remember that domain scores are averages of multiple items, so you need to consider the average across all items in that domain.

3. **Perfect 100% is Unlikely**: Due to the discrete nature of integer responses and decimal occupation values, exactly 100% is theoretically possible but practically difficult to achieve.

4. **This is a Guide, Not a Cheat Sheet**: The assessment is meant to measure your genuine interests, values, and personality. Use this guide to understand what the occupation profile looks like, not to artificially inflate your score.
